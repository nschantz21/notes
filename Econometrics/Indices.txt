Comparing Indicies

Indices are always percent change from one period to another.
If you want to compare indices in their original form you need to change the base years. If you want to compare relative rates of growth (the second derivative) then I don't hink you need to.


Simple Index
    ratio of two variables and that ratio converted to a percentage.  Always like units.
